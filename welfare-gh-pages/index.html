Surplus Food Rescue

We ask local businesses and restauraunts to support the cause by donating food they would otherwise discard of. Through this collective effort, we reduce waste, foster sustainability, and nourish the less privileged.

Our Beneficiaries

We primarily donate to food banks and homeless shelters. If you fall into these categories, please contact us! Shoot us an email at foodflowngo@gmail.com

Efficient Food Relocation

The same day we recieve a shipment food from our sponsors, one of our team members or Edison Academy FBLA chapter members will deliver it to the recipents.

About Us

Under the Middlesex County Academy FBLA Chapter, we aspire to redistribute food - taking it from wehre it is wasted to where it is valued.

Bridging the Gap:

Acting as the middleman between surplus and scarcity by relocating food to where it’s needed most.

About Us

We're a food recovery program centered in Edison, New Jersey. We believe in the power of community, and we're here to redistribute, educate, and empower. Join us in making the world one meal closer to a sustainable, hunger-free future.

Bridging the Gap:

Acting as the middleman between surplus and scarcity by relocating food to where it’s needed most.

Power in Numbers:

Forming a crew of eager volunteers: we’re always looking for more people to join our team.

Joint Effort:

Teaming up with organizations and institutions, pooling strengths to efficiently combat food insecurity and make a lasting difference.

Waste-Free Approach:

Approaching the problem differently: addressing food insecurity by transporting excess, leaving no room for waste in our mission to create a greener, more sustainable tomorrow.

Youthful Impact:

Empowering individuals of all ages as catalysts for change. Join us to prove that age is just a number when it comes to making a difference in the fight against food insecurity.

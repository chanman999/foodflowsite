About Us

Under the Middlesex County Academy FBLA Chapter, we aspire to redistribute food - taking it from where it is wasted to where it is valued.

1. Receiving leftover food from restaurants and other donors.
2. Distributing food to local centers that are in need.

Receiving Food
We reach out to areas that may have leftover food items, such as food chains and bakeries.

Donating Food
We take the collected food to homeless shelters, food banks, and other such locations.

Overall... 0 percent of the donated food goes towards Food Banks and Orphanages!
